Keyboard Services

I offer a keyboard build services starting at $25 usd, but prices vary depending on various factors (e.g. size of keyboard). I can either help source the parts for you depending on the complexity of the project, or you can send the parts to me directly.

For beginners, please fill out this form if interested in this services. this gives me an understanding of your preferences and price range, which will allow me to help source parts for you. I will email back a summary of what your keyboard could look like within those requirements, and once satisfied, I can buy the parts and build the keyboard for you once the buidl invoice is paid.

Otherwise, please email me at galaxeo.justin@gmail.com regarding any inquiries or questions.
